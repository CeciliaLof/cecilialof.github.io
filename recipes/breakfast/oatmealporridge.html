Ingredients:

  • 1dl oatmeal
  • 2dl water
  • a pinch of salt

This is an incredibly simple recipe, but it's filling and very versatile. I usually top with strawberry jam, but you can add fresh berries and why not add some milk?

Instructions

  1. Put everything in a microwave safe bowl.
  2. Microwave for 1 minute.
  3. Stir it around a little.
  4. Microwave for an additional 1 minute.
  5. Top with your favourite topping!
