Today is the day i start working on a new Blender project! I usually just play around and make stuff, but now i want to creat a full game character from start to finish. This means that i have to be happy with the design first before jumping into the 3D stuff.

ðŸ–ŠProject plan breakdown

  1. Sketch out a character design that i like
  2. Illustrate a full body front and side view to be used as a 3D reference.
  3. Model the character! It's important to think of the polycount, it will make the next steps so much easier.
  4. UV unwrap the character.
  5. Texture the character in Substance Painter.
  6. Rig the character, and maybe make a couple of animations or poses to showcase it.
  7. Post it in my projects site!

Follow along with me!

My plan is to post noteworthy updates here on my blog so you can follow along. I will give some tips along the way, such as modeling tips, creative tips and so on. I have learned some things and i'll gladly share them with you!
