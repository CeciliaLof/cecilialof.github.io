The Bitter End (Game)

What is The Bitter End?

The Bitter End was a game project me and two other studends created for the course Future Studies in university, where we had to use two future study methods and apply it to a digital media production area. We chose gaming since it's an interest for us. We picked the study methods Scenario Planning and Trend Analysis.
The game took around three weeks from start to finish, a short timespan for such a ambitious idea. The goal and theme of the study was how AI will be implemented in gaming in the future. We picked a scenario where you could type anything you wanted and the NPC will reply.
This game was just a prototype so no real AI was involved in the gameplay, we instead used ChatGPT to decide what the dialogue would be beforehand as a script.

The Gameplay

You play as a guard tasked to solve a murder. One patron in the bar is the guilty one, you have to talk to them and find clues, and in the end you decide who is the guilty one.


My Contribution

My sole responsibility was to create the 3D assets for the game. Everything from the bottles scattered around the tavern, to the one man squid band playing a jazzy tune. I was also responsible for the overall environment mood, such as shading, lighting and placing the assets.
